Biken Software

ENIB

During our 3rd year at ENIB, I developed with friends a software to generate random bike routes in France. This software uses a pathfinding algorithm to create the best appropriate path. You can choose the distance you want to travel or you can simply define a starting point and a destination point. All this works on OpenStreetMap data.
